What file types does it accept?
PaveView Takeoff accepts PDF files (single and multi-page) and all common image formats — JPG, PNG, TIFF. Any plan you can get your hands on digitally will work.
How accurate are the measurements?
Accuracy depends on your calibration — if you calibrate precisely against the plan's scale bar, measurements are within 1–2% of actual. We've tested against field measurements and professional takeoff services with consistent results.
Do I need to install anything?
No. PaveView Takeoff runs entirely in your web browser. Works on any computer — Windows, Mac, or even a tablet. No installation, no updates to manage.
